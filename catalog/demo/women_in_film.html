Gender Bias In Movies - Do Women Only Talk About Men?
To pass the Bechdel test, a movie has to meet 3 criteria: (1) it has to have at least two women in it, (2) who talk to each other (3) about something besides a man.
Surprisingly, 533 of the 1453 movies (37%) in this dataset fail this gender test! Explore the data below.
