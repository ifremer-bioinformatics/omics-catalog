Photographers’ Identities Catalog
Here is a collection of biographical data describing 110k people involved in the production of photographic images. The information is from trusted biographical dictionaries, catalogs and databases, and from extensive original research by NYPL Photography Collection staff. If you are a historian, student, genealogist, or simply lover of photography's history, explore the data!!
Data From The New York Public Library. NYPL's interface to this data is available at http://pic.nypl.org/.
